However, as a vegetarian, I faced some challenges when it came to finding suitable dining options. Traditional Hawaiian and Filipino cuisine, which predominantly features meat and seafood, posed limitations for my dietary preferences. Despite the occasional struggles with food, the rejuvenating experience of being in my dad's hometown, surrounded by stunning landscapes and embracing the island's laid-back lifestyle, more than made up for it. Finding solace in both city life and the tranquility of nature, this trip allowed me to strike a balance between urban exploration and the revitalizing energy of the natural world.

"A HAZE, A DREAM ALMOST" | 2:37 AM

The theme of "stargazing" evokes a sense of wonder, serenity, and exploration. It transports us to a tranquil night sky, where we gaze up at the vast expanse of stars. The concept of "haze" adds a layer of mystique and enchantment to this theme. It symbolizes the soft, ethereal quality that envelops the celestial scene, creating a dreamlike atmosphere. The haze obscures the details and sharpness of the surroundings, emphasizing the sense of being lost in the cosmic expanse. It invites us to embrace the unknown, to let our imagination wander, and to find solace and inspiration in the limitless beauty of the universe. The theme of "stargazing" with a touch of "haze" awakens a sense of awe and curiosity, encouraging us to pause, reflect, and connect with the vastness of the cosmos.

The quote "A haze, a dream almost" originated from my friend Xae, whose keen interest in astrophysics and philosophy seamlessly intertwine. Xae's fascination with astrophysics allows them to delve into the mysteries of the cosmos, exploring the intricate workings of the universe. Their studies in astrophysics have instilled in them a deep appreciation for the grandeur and awe-inspiring nature of the cosmos. Simultaneously, Xae's passion for philosophy enables them to contemplate the existential questions and ponder the meaning of our existence within the vastness of space. They find that these two fields converge beautifully, with astrophysics offering insights into the physical aspects of the universe and philosophy providing a framework for reflecting on the metaphysical and philosophical implications. The quote they shared, "A haze, a dream almost," encapsulates the ethereal and elusive nature of our cosmic exploration, inviting us to venture into the celestial haze with wonder and curiosity.
